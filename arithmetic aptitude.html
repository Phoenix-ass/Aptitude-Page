Question:

1. A person crosses a 600 m long street in 5 minutes. What is his speed in km per hour?

A. 3.6
B. 7.2
C. 8.4
D. 10
Explanation: Option B
Speed = {600/ 5 x 60} m/sec. = 2 m/sec. Converting m/sec to km/hr. = {2 x 18/5} km/hr. =7.2 km/hr.
